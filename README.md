 Blackjack Game

A simple yet interactive Blackjack card game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Play directly in your browser!

 🎮 Features

- **Start Game** - Deal your initial two cards
- **Hit** - Draw additional cards to get closer to 21
- **Stand** - Stop drawing and reveal your final sum
- **Play Again** - Start a new round instantly without refreshing
- **Score Tracking** - Keep track of your chip count

 🎯 Game Rules

- The goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over
- Face cards (J, Q, K) count as 10
- Aces count as 11 (or 1 if needed to avoid bust)
- If you reach exactly 21, you get Blackjack!
- Going over 21 means you're out of the game (bust)

 🚀 How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click **START GAME** to deal your first two cards
3. Click **NEW CARD** to draw more cards or **STAND** to stop
4. View your sum and messages to track the game state
5. Click **PLAY AGAIN** when the round ends to start over
